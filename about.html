HI, I'M STELLA!

Currently based in Aalborg, while in my first year of studying Media Graphics at Aalborg TECHCOLLEGE, I create designs and art with inspiration from my everyday environment and online media.


It has always been interesting to me to work with Digital Media and Communication and I've had a fair amount of experience from my former fields of educations and side hobbies at home. Whether it would be artwork, UX design, graphics, video content or running social media, I would like to strengthen my skills further within these areas.


Coming all the way from Vietnam and spending most of my life in Denmark, I've adjusted myself to a different environment and culture, which makes me open to understand and explore more new experiences within this world.


I believe that with a curious and open-minded approach to everything, you can adapt and make your contributions to everything more effective and enjoyable.

Designing is something I'd like to pursue as my ideal form of work and personal expression, and getting out to the world with my work is a goal I am determined to fulfill.
