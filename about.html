who am i?

While currently studying Media Graphics at TECHCOLLEGE in Aalborg, I create designs and art that take inspiration from my every day life and external media. Graphic Design is something I'd like to pursue as my ideal form of work and personal expression and I hope to be able to show what I can do. Coming all the way from Vietnam, I've adapted myself to a different environment and culture, which makes me open to understand and learn more new experiences within this big world.
